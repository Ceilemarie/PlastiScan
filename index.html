PLASTISCAN

Scan your plastic waste and find out how to recycle it!

About Us

PlastiScan is a project that aims to help people identify and recycle their plastic waste more effectively. By using a simple scanning technology, users can quickly determine the type of plastic they have and receive information on how to recycle it properly.

Our Goal

Our goal is to reduce plastic waste and promote recycling by providing an easy-to-use tool that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their plastic consumption. We believe that by increasing awareness and accessibility, we can contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable environment.

Instructions

To use PlastiScan, simply follow these steps:

  1. Download the PlastiScan app from your app store.
  2. Open the app and allow it to access your camera.
  3. Scan the plastic item you want to recycle by pointing your camera at it.
  4. The app will identify the type of plastic and provide you with recycling instructions specific to that type.
  5. Follow the instructions to recycle your plastic waste properly.
