PlastiScan

Scan your plastic waste and find out how to recycle it!

About Us

We are Grade 12 STEM students from Bolbok Integrated National High School who developed the PLASTISCAN, a solar-powered plastic bottle recycling vending machine with RFID card recognition. Our team aims to combine technology and environmental responsibility by creating an automated system that encourages students to recycle plastic bottles inside the school campus. Through this project, we promote discipline, cleanliness, and innovation while helping reduce plastic waste and protecting the environment.

Our Goal

The goal of our project is to promote proper waste disposal and increase student participation in recycling by making the process easy, interactive, and rewarding. Instead of throwing plastic bottles anywhere, students are encouraged to recycle them using the machine and earn points. This helps the school maintain a cleaner environment, builds eco-friendly habits among students, and supports sustainable practices using modern technology and renewable solar energy.

Instructions

  1. Student must first tap their RFID card on the scanner to register their identity.
  2. After the system recognizes the user, they can insert an empty plastic bottle into the bottle slot.
  3. The machine will automatically detect and count the bottle, then display the earned points on the screen.
  4. The app will identify the type of plastic and provide you with recycling instructions specific to that type.
  5. Once finished, the student can tap their card again to confirm and save their total points.
  6. The collected bottles are stored inside the machine for proper recycling.

Equivalent of each bottle & Rewards

Each plastic bottle has a corresponding point value based on its size.

350 mL Bottle

1 Point

Small plastic bottles are equal to 1 point each.

500 mL Bottle

1.5 Points

Medium plastic bottles are equal to 1.5 points each.

1000 mL Bottle

2 Points

Large plastic bottles are equal to 2 points each.

The points are automatically saved in the student's RFID account after the bottle is accepted by the machine. Students can accumulate points and exchange them for rewards such as school supplies, snacks, or other incentives prepared by the school. This system motivates students to recycle more plastic bottles while helping keep the school clean and environmentally friendly.
